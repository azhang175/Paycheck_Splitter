## BudgetFlow

A desktop application that helps you split your money across multiple accounts (no calculator needed).

---

## Features

- Calculate how much goes to each account based on a fixed dollar amount or a percentage
- Add, rename, edit, and delete accounts
- Automatically saves your accounts to a local files so you don't have to set it up every time

---
## Requirements 

- Python 3.7 or higher
- No additional installs needed (Tkinter comes built into Python)

---
## How to Run

1. Go to [Releases](https://github.com/azhang175/BudgetFlow/releases)
2. Download the lastest 'BudgetFlow.exe'
3. Double-click to launch the app

---
## How to Use

1. Click **Add Account**
2. Enter the account name and the amount (fixed dollar amount or percentage)
3. Click **Create** — repeat for each account you want to add
4. Click **Done** when you are finished adding accounts
5. Enter your total paycheck amount
6. Click **Calculate** to see how your money is split

---
